REMAIN FOCUSED & BOOST ENERGY WITH MATCHA GREEN TEA.

Matcha is the powdered form of the green tea plant (camellia sinensis) and has been consumed in Japan for centuries due to its near mythical health giving properties. The Matchaletic team source premium grade matcha from Japan and promote its use amongst athletes and fitness fanatics. Our vision is to develop a range of sports and health products exploiting the powerful properties of matcha green tea.

Incorporating matcha into your everyday diet may help you lose fat without compromising your energy levels and focus. We know that modern life can make it difficult to maintain a healthy lifestyle. Matcha will give you the energy and focus you require to become an #EverydayAthlete.

You can visit our ever growing knowledge base for more info on matcha and its many health benefits or just feel free to have a look round our site. If there’s anything you’d like to ask, please do!

SUSTAINED FOCUS

Matcha contains the amino acid l-theanine which acts immediately across the blood - brain barrier to increase focus.

INCREASED ENERGY

The caffeine in matcha combined with l-theanine promotes focused energy which lasts for several hours after consumption.

BURN FAT

The antioxidant EGCG contained in abundance in matcha has been shown to promote fat burning in published studies.
